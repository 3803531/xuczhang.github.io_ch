About Me

I am a Ph.D. student of Computer Science Department at Discovery Analytic Center in Virginia Tech. My advisor is Prof. Chang-Tien Lu. I received my B.E. degree in Software Engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2009. Before joining Virginia Tech, I worked for Microsoft and SAP Inc. as software engineer and technical support engineer. My research interests include distributed robust learning, predictive models and robust models in deep learning.
